Notion Embeds

Integrate your Google Calendar directly into Notion! Notion Embeds are embeddable pages that blend perfectly into Notion.

1

Choose an embed

Right now the only option is a view of your Google Calendar events for the day. (If you like this one, I'll add more!)

2

Copy the embed URL

3

Create the embed in Notion

Open your favorite notion page and use the /embed command to create a new embed. Paste the URL and voilà!
